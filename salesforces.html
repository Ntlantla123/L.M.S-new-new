Salesforce



History

Salesforce was founded in 1999 by Marc Benioff, a sales executive at Oracle, one of the largest software companies in the world. The company was founded on a single, bold premise that software should be made available to the masses, on a 24/7 basis, over a global cloud computing infrastructure.
